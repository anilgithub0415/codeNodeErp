Recommendation for Educational Software:
Given the nature of educational software, which often involves:

Real-time collaboration: Group chats, collaborative whiteboards, shared document editing.
Live interactive lessons/quizzes: Instant feedback, polling.
Notifications: Updates on assignments, announcements.
Varying user network conditions: Students might be connecting from home, schools with restrictive networks.
Need for reliability: You can't afford to lose a student's quiz answer or a teacher's message.
Developer productivity: As a solo developer or a small team, you want to build features quickly, not re-implement low-level networking concerns.
Socket.IO is the clear winner.
